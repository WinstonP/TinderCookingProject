Quick & Easy Spaghetti with Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 2 tbsp. water
  • ⅓ cup seasoned dry bread crumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces of cooked spaghetti pasta

Directions
  1. Mix the ground beef, water, bread crumbs and egg. Shape into 12 (2 inch) meatballs.
  2. Arrange the meatballs in a microwavable baking dish.
  3. Microwave on HIGH for 5 minutes or until they’re fully cooked.
  4. Stir the sauce in the dish. Cover and microwave for 3 minutes or until hot.
  5. Pour over the cooked spaghetti pasta.
